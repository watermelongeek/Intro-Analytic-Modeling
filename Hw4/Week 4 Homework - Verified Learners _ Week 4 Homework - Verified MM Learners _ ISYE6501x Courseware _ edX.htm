Week 4 Homework - Verified Learners

INSTRUCTIONS

  • Every learner should submit his/her own homework solutions.  However, you are allowed to discuss the homeworks with each other (in fact, I encourage you to form groups and/or use the forums) but everyone must submit his/her own solution; you may not copy someone elses solution.
  • The homework will be peer-graded. In analytics modeling, there are often lots of different approaches that work well, and I want you to see not just your own, but also others.
  • The homework grading scale reflects the fact that the primary purpose of homework is learning:
